About

Daffodil International College (DIC) is recognized in independent government assessments as one of top graded colleges in Bangladesh. The college has been founded by Daffodil Group with the approval of the Ministry of Education under the Private college Act of 1992 and its amendment in 1998 and Daffodil International college came into being on 24th January 2002, the college today combines impressive modern facilities and a dynamic approach to teaching and research with its proud heritage of service and achievement.
